Texas Home Living (TxHL)

This program is designed to provide in-home services to persons who need assistance but who want to maintain the independence of a home setting.

Texas Home Living provides selected essential services and supports to people with intellectual and developmental disabilities who live in their family homes or their own homes. Services provided are in areas such as in home and out of home support, respite, day activity, speech, physical and occupational therapy, psychology, nursing, dental, adaptive aids, supported employment and assistance in finding competitive employment.

Criteria for qualifying:

  • Have a developmental disability
    (for example, Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities, autism, cerebral palsy)
  • Receive funding after being on waiting list
