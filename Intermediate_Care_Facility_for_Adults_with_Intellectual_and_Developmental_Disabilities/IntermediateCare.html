Intermediate Care Facility for Adults with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities

The Intermediate Care Facility for Adults with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities program is a long-term care facility for adults. We have 3 different facilities that are ICF. Two of the programs are housed in 6-unit apartment complexes. This allows for our residents to be in the least restrictive environment, while still having 24-hour supervision and staff assistance. The other program is a 6-bedroom group home for women who need more hands on assistance.

All programs include:

  • case management
  • nursing
  • staff assistance and supervision
  • bookkeeping
  • independent living skills classes
  • transportation
  • assistance with meal preparation
  • taking medications
  • hygiene

Criteria for qualifying:

  • Over the age of 18
  • Have a developmental disability (for example Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities, autism, cerebral palsy
  • No uncontrollable behavior
  • No risk of elopement
